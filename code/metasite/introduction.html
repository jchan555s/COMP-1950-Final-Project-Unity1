Introduction

This is the proposal for the redesign of BCIT's COMP 1950 webpage. Our client: Jeff Parker would like to have the COMP 1950 website redesigned to better meet the students' needs. The main function of the webpage is to allow students to have easy access to the lecture material, exercises, and resources.

The redesign will have a new navigation menu to let students access the course material quickly and efficiently. New features such as online quizzes, message board, real-time survey will increase classroom partipation and collaboration. The website will come complete with a new and refreshing style that will make learning COMP1950 fun and exciting.

The web design team of Sean Johnson and Jason Chan will ensure that the website redesign will meet and exceed the students' of COMP 1950 expectations.
