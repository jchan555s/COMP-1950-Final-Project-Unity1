Introduction

This is the proposal for the redesign of BCIT's COMP 1950 webpage. Our client: Jeff Parker would like to redesign the website to better meet the needs of the students. The main function of the webpage is to allow students to have easy access to the lecture material, exercises, and resources.

The redesign will have new features such as online quizes and possibily online submission for homework as well as the feedback from the teacher directly associated with the submission. The website will come complete with a new and refreshing style that will make learning COMP1950 fun and exciting.

The web design team of Sean Johnson and Jason Chan will ensure that the website redesign will meet and exceed Jeff Parker's expectation.
